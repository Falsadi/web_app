Phishing

A scam where attackers pretend to be a trusted person or company (like a bank or email provider) to trick you into giving away personal info like passwords or credit card numbers

Ransomware

A type of malware that locks or encrypts your files, then demands money (a ransom) to unlock them

Trojan Horse

A malicious program that looks safe or helpful, but once you open or install it, it secretly lets hackers into your computer
